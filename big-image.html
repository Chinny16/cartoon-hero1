The Life is Eazi Concert: Falz the Bahd Guy, Reminisce, Lil Kesh, Efya, Maleek Berry, Ajebutter 22, Pappy Kojo and Others Perform+ Highlights from the Event

December 22, 2016 0 Comments
  • Celebrity
  • Celebrity News
  • ENTERTAINMENT
Pin It

“Life is Eazi, Lagos” with Mr Eazi & Friends was a sold out concert which took place on Wednesday 21st of December, 2016 at the Landmark Event Centre. The concert powered by Kamdora’s sister company Betazoo saw Mr Eazi who came on the scene of the Afrobeats music industry less than one year ago selling out his Lagos show.

Considering the amount of people who showed up either through regular or VIP ticket, and the VVIP Personnel tables who booked out the lounge, you wouldn’t even know there were at least 3 other events going on in Lagos.  Davido held Industry Nite Grand Finale, Black Coffee the BET Award winner was playing a set nearby, and also Reekado Banks of Mavin Records Thank you Concert at the New Afrika Shrine were all happening on the same night. It proves to show Mr Eazi has already built a loyal fan base in Lagos and can also boast of having numerous fans outside of Nigeria in Ghana, Uk, America and much more.

This breakthrough artist of 2016 has had more hit songs such as Dance For Me, Skintight, Hollup, Anointing, Bankulize and others in one year due to hard work, consistency, brand identity, social media presence and in my humble opinion God’s grace than other Afrobeats artist who have even been around longer than him. He has worked with top individuals in Nigeria, his adopted home Ghana and also outside Africa with people like Eugy, Lil Kesh, Efya, Phyno, DJ Spinall, DJ Neptune, Reminisce, Reekado Banks, Illbliss and others.

Mr Eazi and his friends such as upcoming and established Nigerian artists like Giftty, Poe, Fumbi, Maleek Berry, Ajebutter, Falz The Bahd Guy, Illbliss, Reminisce,  Mayorkun, Lil Kesh, Phyno; and his Ghanian squad such as Pappy Kojo, Joey B the man behind famous Ghanian track “Tonga” and also Efya. We also had comedians  Kenny Blaq and skit actor behind Mama Tobi and Oluwakaponeski real name Emmanuel Samuel in attendance. 

Oh yeah, let’s not forget the night MC MTV Base Presenter Ehiz and DJs who kept the night going such as DJ Neptune, DJ Cuppy, DJ Kakip and others. Mr Eazi Celebrity friends such as DJ Spinall, VJ Adams, Bizzle, Pulse Presenter Chuey Chu, Skinny Girl in Transit Tiwa (real name Abimbola Craig), Celebrity Stylist Swanky Jerry and others were present. The crowd was peaceful and young. These are definitely the coolest and freshest fans have ever seen. Some were even spotted wearing his signature raffia hat.
